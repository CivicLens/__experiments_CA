emergency permit and limited assignment permit which the employing agency estimates, based on previous year actual needs
and projections of enrollment, it will need during the year covered by the Declaration. The Declaration may be revised at a later

Goals :

4.0 Focus Area: Efficient Utilization of Fiscal Capital

Public Content
DESCRIPTION: The Board of Education approved Resolution 13-20-21 on September 24, 2020, adopting the PARS
Supplementary Retirement Plan, a retirement incentive offer for eligible employees. Additionally, on January 21, 2021, the Board
of Education voted final approval of the SRP based on the determination that the enrollment of the plan met the Districts fiscal
and operational objectives. The goal of the program is to generate savings by increasing the number of retirements in the 20202021 school year. The SRP provides participating employees with a monthly benefit provided by 70% of their final year salary
paid into the plan over a five-year period. The District has determined that allowing additional enrollments into the SRP will
generate additional savings for the District and therefore meets the Districts fiscal and operational objectives.
FISCAL IMPACT: The Supplementary Retirement Plan (SRP) is designed to generate savings for the District. The District has
determined that allowing additional enrollments into the SRP will generate additional savings for the District and therefore meets
the Districts fiscal and operational objectives.
