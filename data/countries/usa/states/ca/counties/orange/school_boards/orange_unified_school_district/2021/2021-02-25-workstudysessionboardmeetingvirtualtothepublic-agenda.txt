Public Content
DESCRIPTION:California School Boards Association (CSBA), Region 15, is holding elections for the 2021Delegate
Assembly. The Board as a whole votes for up to the number of vacancies in the region. Region 15 has 9vacancies. The Board
may cast no more than one vote for any one candidate and for no more than9 candidates.The Board has received the
biographical information on the candidates as provided by California School Boards Association.
The official ballot must be postmarked on or before March 15. The names of all Delegates will be available on CSBAs website no
later than April 1. Delegates serve two-year terms beginning April 1, 2021 March 31, 2023. They meet twice a year to conduct
business and may also meet with the other delegates and the director within their region.

In alignment with the OUSD EDGE Strategic Plan, the Board of Education will discuss and identify future priorities and goals for

Excellence in Academics & Leadership
Dedicated & Engaged Communications

Genuine Wellness and Safety
