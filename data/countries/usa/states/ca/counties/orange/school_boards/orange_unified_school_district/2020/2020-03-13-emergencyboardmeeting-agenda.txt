Recommended Action :

It is recommended that the Board of Education approve the
closure/dismissal of schools for student attendance within
Orange Unified School District beginning March 16 through
March 27.

Public Content
DESCRIPTION: The District is working on provisions for nutrition and childcare services while school is
dismissed. Staff will continue to work during this period of emergency unless otherwise notified. There will
be a plan of action to address online student learning.
