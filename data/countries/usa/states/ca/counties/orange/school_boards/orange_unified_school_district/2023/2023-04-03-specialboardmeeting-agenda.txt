A. Selection of Executive Search Firm for Hiring A
New Superintendent

Meeting :

Apr 3, 2023 - Special Board Meeting

Category :

3. INFORMATION/DISCUSSION/ACTION ITEM

Type :

Action, Discussion, Information

Public Content
DESCRIPTION: The Board of Education will retain a consultant to conduct a searchfor the new successorsuperintendent to
include external and internal applicants. The Board authorized staff to request proposals from professional search firms that
conduct recruitmentsfor the position of Superintendent.
The District received a total of six proposals to be considered by the Board of Education:
* Hazard, Young, Attea & Associates
* LINQM Inc.
* McPherson & Jacobson
* Stanton Chase
* Summit Search Solutions, Inc.
* WorldBridge Partners
Each firm will have 20 minutes to give a presentation and have an opportunity to respond to questions from the Trustees.

FISCAL IMPACT: To be determined upon selection of firm and disclosed at the April 13, 2023, regular board meeting.

Motion & Voting
It is recommended that the board brings back McPherson & Jacobson and Hazard, Young, Attea to the
April 13th, 2023, meeting for a vote.
Motion by Angie Rumsey, second by Madison Miner.
Final Resolution: Motion carries
Yea: Andrea Yamasaki, Kris Erickson, Ana Page, Rick Ledesma, John Ortega, Angie Rumsey, Madison
Miner
