Focus on 4 C’s – Communication, Collaboration, Creativity and Critical Thinking
Hands on learning and exploration
High academic standards for all students and utilize learnings from Covid to focus on continuous

Build relationships with community to increase trust and transparency and commit to civility and open
debate
Genuine Wellness and Safety
Every student welcomed and respected, build trust and respect for all
Increase counseling efforts to support graduate profile
Expand Social/emotional learning and inclusiveness
Efficient Utilization of Fiscal Capital
Expand focus on 21st century classrooms – furniture and designs
Utilize technology in 1-1 environment to enhance learning
