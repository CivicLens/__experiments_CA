Board President, Kathy Moffat presented an award to Dr. Hansen. The OCSBA Maureen DiMarco Award was

County School Board's Association (OCSBA) an association of local school districts and community college boards,
has handed out this award since 1992. Kathy Moffat stated, tonight, I am honored to announce that Superintendent
Hansen, along with the other 27 Orange County Superintendents were bestowed with this prestigious award "In
Recognition of Exemplary Leadership for Youth and the Education Community amidst a Global health Pandemic". The
Orange County School Boards Association recognized Superintendent Hansen's vision and passion in addressing the
myriad of needs for our diverse students and families district-wide, which led to the successful implementation of our
OUSD Slice Instructional Models, as well as the execution of our Safety & Health Driven School Reopening Plan.
Board President, Kathy Moffat stated, "Dr. Hansen, on behalf of the Board of Education, your Executive Cabinet, and
the staff, students and parents of our OUSD families, I present you with this award and thank you for your continued
modeling of our District Core Values of Integrity, Equity, Respect, and Excellence in everything you say and do."
Dr. Hansen was surprised and very appreciative, she thanked everyone and said, "It Takes a Team."

continue to support our schools, staff and students with fundraising and special events in ways we never
imagined. Teacher appreciation week is this week and the support groups are committed to supporting the staff at our
district schools as they do each year.
