Information, Reports: B. Board President's Report
Board President Kathy Moffat spoke about how the members of the community have all been affected by what is
happening with the division in the United States, and asked that the board and those watching think about the
Pledge of Allegiance, and remember that it is a gift to live in this country, and we need to treasure this gift. She also

theme "Representation, Identify and Diversity". Our students are studying in the classroom about the great leaders,

the heroes of the past, the struggle for equality and the quest to keep their dreams alive. She also shared that two of
our wonderful parent volunteers have been honored by the Orange County Department of Education with the 2021
Orange County K-12 School Counselor Advocate Award. Nicol Jones and Kathy White are the recipients of
the "School Counselor Advocate Award" for working tirelessly to support and advocate for the school counseling
profession and support the implementation of a comprehensive school counseling program, including wellness centers
to enhance student wellness and mental health in Orange Unified. Thank you to Katherine White and Nicol Jones for
their continued hard work.
