4. ACTION ITEMS
Subject :

A. Resolution to Extend the Closure of All Orange
Unified School District Schools to May 8, 2020.

Meeting :

Mar 24, 2020 - Special Emergency Board Meeting

Category :

4. ACTION ITEMS

Type :

Action

Recommended Action :

It is recommended that the Board of Education approve the
continuation of the closure/dismissal of schools for student
attendance within Orange Unified School District through
Friday, May 8, 2020.

Public Content
DESCRIPTION: In light ofGovernor Gavin Newsom's executive orderand thelatest guidance from local
health officials, a number of Orange County districts have pushed back their student dismissal dates until
late April or May. Orange Unified will extend the student dismissal until Friday, May 8, 2020. Students are
scheduled to return Monday, May 11.
In a statement, County Superintendent Dr. Al Mijaresannounced his supportfor extending student
dismissals based on the Governor's Order and current social distancing guidelines. Meanwhile, Orange
Unified School District will continue to build out distance learning plans and lessons for students. Grab
and Go Meals will be available and devices and hotspots continue to be distributed to students who need
them during the closure period. During the scheduled Spring Break, March 30-April 3, these services will
not be available.
