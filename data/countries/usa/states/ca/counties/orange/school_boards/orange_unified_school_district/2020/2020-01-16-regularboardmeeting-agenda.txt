DESCRIPTION: A proposed student calendar for the 2021-22school year and a proposed amendment to the Board approved
student calendar for the 2020-21school year, both incorporating an 80/100 semester model for high school students, are to be

item.

The parameters used in the construction of the proposed 2021-22school year and a proposed amendment to the Board
approved student calendar for the 2020-21school yearareconsistent with the design of the current Board approved student
calendars, including an 80/100 semester model for high schools,a 180 day student schedule, a one week recess at

one week recess at Spring Break.
The proposed student calendars in this item have been reviewed by the Executive Cabinet following their completion by the
District Student Calendar Committee.The District Student Calendar Committee is comprised of representatives from elementary,
middle, and high schools, several District departments, as well as each of the two employee associations.
The proposed student calendars in this item meet State requirements (California Education Code Sections 46200- 46201) for the
number of school days and instructional minutes for the school year.
