DESCRIPTION: The Board of Education has requested a Work Study Session to receive information regarding the ongoing
Facilities Master Plan.This evening Architects from LPA, Inc. (Board approved July 25, 2019) will be presenting an update on the
Comprehensive Facilities Master Plan to date. The facilities master plan is scheduled to be completed in September 2020, this
presentation will serve as an overview of findings to date by LPA, Inc.
Development of a current District-wide facilities master plan will be used as a planning tool and assist the District in determining
facility improvement needs for the next decade, inclusive of short and long-range planning to meet the changing needs of
students, and to help ensure that educational goals and resources are allocated in an efficient and effective manner to meet a
common coordinated vision. The facilities master plan will include a 10 year projection of demographics, educational
specifications, capacity analysis of schools sites, and recommendations for modernization of school sites.
